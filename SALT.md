### getting started

## commands

- b - `cargo build` - builds salt binary
- i - `cp target/debug/salt /usr/local/bin/s` - installs the salt binary
- r - `cargo run` - installs the salt binary
- c - `cargo clippy` - runs clippy command for salt binary
